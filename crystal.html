Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random numbner at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clickin on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your totak score goes above the random numnber.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game start, the game will change the values of each crystal.
